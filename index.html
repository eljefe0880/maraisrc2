The2nd

Welcome to The Second Running Club based in the 2nd arrondissement where passion for running meets the charm of Paris' 2nd arrondissement! Our club, affectionately known as "The Second," is more than just a group of runners—we're a community that celebrates the pulse-intense and vibrant districts of Paris. Founded in 2025, we've quickly become a fixture in the local fitness scene, bringing together runners of all levels, from beginners to seasoned marathoners.
